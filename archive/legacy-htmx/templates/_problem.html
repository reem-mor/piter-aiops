What I'm solving

The problem

Alerts fire 24/7. On-call engineers spend the first 5–15 minutes hunting for the right runbook, past ticket, or post-mortem — that's pure business impact.

The idea

Feed all runbooks, alert histories and post-mortems into a Bedrock Knowledge Base. When an alert hits, ask a question and get a grounded, cited answer in seconds.

Why it matters

Lower MTTR, less tribal knowledge, faster onboarding for new on-call. Same RAG pattern companies use for internal support and SRE assistants.
